Background

Formerly a high school math teacher, I pursue clarity in understanding and communicating the technical concepts found all around us. I view writing code as an art of simplifying the complex.

I started writing code as a student in Harvard's CS50, which I took as an elective course while working on a graduate degree in "Math for Teaching." At the same time, I dove into "Uncle Bob's" book Clean Code, finding joy and ultimately realizing that programming was the field for me.

I thrive on the challenge of refining concepts and crafting 'clean code.'
